About OneKinship

One Kinship LLC.

I am an online fitness coach! What does this mean exactly? I want to help people be the best they can be without unrealistic expectations. My name is Don Frisone and I am the CEO of One Kinship.

About 4 years ago I weighed 380 pounds. I was over 65% body fat and morbidly obese. I was on the verge of diabetes and hypertrophic cardiomyopathy. I was in a bad spot and at 28 years old, Doctors were saying I would need heart surgery by the time I was 32. They said I would need to change my ways or I would be on a whole host of medications for the rest of my life. At this time in my life I was depressed, lazy, and barely holding on. The only thing kept me going was my wife and the birth of my daughter. I lost my father when I was 17 years old to heart disease, and you’d think I would know better. Instead, I was far worse.

One Kinship is being built on these foundations. I want to provide a place people can come to achieve their goals. If you are looking to lose weight, build muscle, improve your athletic ability, or just be healthier, you are at the right place.

Take a look around the site. I am here to help others become the best version of themselves. I am not saying it will be easy, it won’t happen overnight, but I will be here as a resource and as a support to get you to your goals. Take it from someone who lost over 180 pounds, you can do this and I will help show you the path!

Why Choose Me?

Congratulations on making it this far. You're probably thinking that you'd like to make some changes to your lifestyle, you don't have enough time, it's too hard. It’s time to realize that you do have the time. All you need is as little as 30 minutes a day and the right meal plan. I won't lie to you. Tt will be hard, but not too hard. Your body and your mind are two amazing things, and for too long you have told it "I can't". Now it's time to tell it "I can" all it takes is finding your WHY.

Why do you want to make a change now? Your WHY must be stronger than your CAN’T. I promise that you will have someone with you every step of the way. We will make mistakes, we will fail, but we will get back up and we will succeed. We won't let a bad choice turn into a bad day or a bad month. Together we can do anything! Giving me a chance I will show you that for limited financial burden, a fraction of your 24 hour day and a promise of you giving it your all, we can make anything happen.
